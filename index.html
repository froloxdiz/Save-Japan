Turning Abandoned Houses into Spooky Tourist Spots!

About Our Project 🕷️

Our Mission 🎃

We're students who want to save Japan's abandoned houses! There are millions of empty homes across the country, and we have a cool idea to turn them into haunted house attractions.

By combining Japanese ghost stories with tourism, we can help rural areas make money again and preserve old buildings that would otherwise fall apart.

Why This Matters 🦇

Japan has over 8 million abandoned houses called "akiya". Many are in beautiful rural areas that are losing population.

Our plan creates jobs, brings tourists, and saves historic buildings. Plus, Japan has awesome ghost folklore that tourists love!

The Problem 🏚️

🏚️

Too Many Empty Houses

Over 8.5 million houses are abandoned in Japan as young people move to big cities.

👴

Aging Population

Elderly owners pass away and nobody wants to take care of old family homes.

💰

No Money in Rural Areas

Empty houses make property values drop and towns lose income.

🏛️

Losing History

Beautiful traditional buildings are crumbling and historical architecture is disappearing.

⚠️

Safety Issues

Old broken buildings are dangerous and can attract crime.

🌾

Ghost Towns

Entire villages are dying out with no shops or people left.

Our Solution 🎃

BEFORE 😢

🏚️

Broken, abandoned, wasting away. Nobody wants them and they hurt the community.

AFTER 🎉

👻

Exciting tourist attraction! Bringing money and life back to towns.

💰 Make Money

Ticket sales and tourists spending money in local shops and restaurants.

👥 Create Jobs

Actors, tour guides, maintenance workers all needed!

🏛️ Save History

Fix up old buildings instead of letting them rot.

🌏 Attract Tourists

People from all over the world love Japanese ghost stories.

🎭 Share Culture

Teach visitors about yokai and traditional folklore.

🏘️ Revive Towns

Bring energy and people back to dying villages.
