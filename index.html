Our Mission üéÉ

We're students who want to save Japan's abandoned houses! There are millions of empty homes across the country, and we have a cool idea to turn them into haunted house attractions.

By combining Japanese ghost stories with tourism, we can help rural areas make money again and preserve old buildings that would otherwise fall apart.

Why This Matters ü¶á

Japan has over 8 million abandoned houses called "akiya". Many are in beautiful rural areas that are losing population.

Our plan creates jobs, brings tourists, and saves historic buildings. Plus, Japan has awesome ghost folklore that tourists love!
