About Our Project 🌕

We're a passionate group aiming to save Japan's abandoned houses. Across the country, thousands of homes stand empty, silent witnesses to population decline. Our idea? Turn them into haunted house attractions — immersive experiences that celebrate Japanese folklore while revitalizing local tourism.

By blending creativity, storytelling, and a touch of fear, we’re bringing life (and screams) back to forgotten places. 🎃
