About Our Project

Our Mission

We're a student-led initiative addressing Japan's abandoned house crisis. With over 8 million empty homes across the country, we've developed an innovative solution to transform these properties into haunted house attractions.

By combining Japanese ghost stories with sustainable tourism, we help rural areas generate revenue and preserve historic buildings that would otherwise deteriorate.

Why This Matters

Japan faces a critical demographic challenge with millions of "akiya" (abandoned houses) scattered across beautiful rural areas experiencing population decline.

Our approach creates employment opportunities, attracts international tourists, and preserves architectural heritage. Japan's rich folklore tradition provides the perfect foundation for authentic cultural experiences.
