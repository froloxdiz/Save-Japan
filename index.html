About Our Project

Our Mission

We're a student-led initiative addressing Japan's abandoned house crisis. With over 8 million empty homes across the country, we've developed an innovative solution to transform these properties into haunted house attractions.

By combining Japanese ghost stories with sustainable tourism, we help rural areas generate revenue and preserve historic buildings that would otherwise deteriorate.

Why This Matters

Japan faces a critical demographic challenge with millions of "akiya" (abandoned houses) scattered across beautiful rural areas experiencing population decline.

Our approach creates employment opportunities, attracts international tourists, and preserves architectural heritage. Japan's rich folklore tradition provides the perfect foundation for authentic cultural experiences.

The Problem

🏚️

Abandoned Houses Crisis

Over 8.5 million houses sit empty across Japan as younger generations migrate to urban centers for employment opportunities.

👴

Aging Demographics

Elderly property owners pass away without successors willing or able to maintain traditional family homes.

💰

Economic Decline

Abandoned properties lower property values and eliminate tax revenue for struggling rural municipalities.

🏛️

Cultural Heritage Loss

Historic traditional architecture is deteriorating, erasing centuries of craftsmanship and cultural identity.

⚠️

Safety Concerns

Deteriorating structures pose safety hazards and can become magnets for illegal activities.

🌾

Community Dissolution

Entire villages face extinction as essential services close and social networks collapse.

Our Solution

BEFORE

🏚️

Deteriorating, abandoned structures that drain community resources and lower property values.

AFTER

⛩️

Thriving cultural attractions generating revenue and bringing vitality back to rural communities.

💰 Revenue Generation

Ticket sales and tourist spending at local businesses create sustainable income streams.

👥 Employment Creation

Performers, guides, maintenance staff, and hospitality workers find meaningful work.

🏛️ Heritage Preservation

Historic buildings receive restoration and ongoing maintenance instead of falling into ruin.

🌏 Tourism Development

International visitors discover authentic Japanese folklore and cultural traditions.

🎭 Cultural Education

Guests learn about yokai mythology and traditional storytelling practices.

🏘️ Community Revitalization

New energy and economic activity breathe life back into declining villages.
